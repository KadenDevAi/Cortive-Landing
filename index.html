How it works?

Get started with Cortive in 3 simple steps

1

Download & Install

Download Cortive for Windows 10/11. Apple support coming soon. Setup takes less than 2 minutes with zero configuration required.

2

Install Browser Extension

Add our browser extension to capture web content. Highlight text or save full pages - the extension sends everything to your desktop app automatically. You can also search your web captures directly from the browser.

3

Search & Discover

The desktop app handles all processing, indexing, and search. Find anything instantly with natural language queries - all happening locally on your machine.

Frequently Asked Questions

Cortive runs entirely on your Windows 10/11 computer using local AI models and databases. Nothing is sent to external servers - all processing, indexing, and search happens locally for complete privacy. Your data stays on your machine, always.

Currently available for Windows 10 and Windows 11. Apple/macOS support is planned for the future. We're focusing on delivering the best possible experience on Windows first before expanding to other platforms.

Cortive doesn't compete with your note-taking apps - it enhances them. While Obsidian and Notion are creation tools, Cortive is a unified search and memory layer that captures knowledge from everywhere, not just one app. It's the missing piece that connects all your scattered information.
