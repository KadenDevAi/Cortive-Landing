What happens after the 100 spots are filled?

Once the 100 Founder's Edition licenses are sold, they are gone forever. Cortive will then move to a standard monthly/annual subscription model for all new users to ensure a sustainable business that can be supported and improved for years to come.

Why isn't Cortive open-source?

This is a strategic decision based on focus and sustainability. As a solo founder, my mission is to build a polished and stable product as quickly as possible. Open-sourcing a project correctly requires a massive overhead in community management that would slow down core development. To ensure Cortive has a long-term future with dedicated support, it needs a viable business model from day one. Trust is built on verifiable user control: your data never leaves your machine and is stored in a standard SQLite file you can see and access at any time.

When is the Mac version coming?

The "Founder's Edition" launch is for Windows. A native macOS version is our highest priority post-launch. Revenue from the Founder's Circle will be directly reinvested into accelerating the development of the Mac version.
